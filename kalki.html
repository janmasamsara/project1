Prabhas

Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju (born 23 October 1979), known as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Telugu cinema.

Deepika Padukone

Deepika Padukone (born 5 January 1986) is an Indian actress who works in Hindi films. She is India's highest-paid actress, and includes three Filmfare Awards.

Nag Ashwin

Nag Ashwin Reddy is an Indian film director known for his works in Telugu cinema. He made his directorial debut in 2015 .
